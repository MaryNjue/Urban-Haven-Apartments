About us

Urban Haven Apartments, located in the serene neighborhood of Kiharu, Murang'a, has been a trusted name in the house renting business for over 12 years. Our mission is to offer high-quality, affordable living spaces that cater to the diverse needs of individuals, couples, and families alike. Whether you're looking for a cozy single room or a spacious two-bedroom self-contained apartment, Urban Haven ensures you experience comfort, privacy, and modern amenities. We pride ourselves on creating a community where convenience meets lifestyle, offering amenities such as free WiFi, a gym, and a community theater, all aimed at making your stay as enjoyable as possible. Join us at Urban Haven Apartments and discover your perfect living space today.
